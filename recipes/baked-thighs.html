Crispy Baked Chicken Thighs

A delicious plate of seasoned, crispy chicken thighs.

These chicken thighs are seasoned to perfection and baked in the oven, resulting in a crunchy exterior while remaining delicious and juicy on the inside.

The best thing about this recipe (Aside from how good it is!) is that it is incredibly simple to make.

Ingredients:

  • 3 Pounds Chicken Thighs
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 Tsp Salt
  • 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
  • 2 Tsp Onion Powder
  • 2 Tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1 Tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 Tsp Paprika
  • (Optional) 1 Tsp Chili Powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Line a large baking sheet with foil for easy cleanup and set aside.
  2. Remove the chicken thighs from their packaging and pat them dry with paper towels.
  3. Arrange the chicken thighs on prepared baking sheet and drizzle with the olive oil, rubbing the oil into the chicken to coat.
  4. In a small bowl mix together the salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, Italian seasoning, and paprika.
  5. Sprinkle the spices over the chicken evenly.
  6. Bake chicken in preheated oven for 45 minutes.*
